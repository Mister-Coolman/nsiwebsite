The Niskayuna Summer Invitational Statistics Team (Arjun R and Daniel Y) aim to get statistics out as soon as possible after the conclusion of the competition. The statistics will be embedded here, along with the top scorers from the round robin stage for each category. The statistics being tracked include both total, and category-specific points per game, interrupts, bonus conversion percentage, average buzzes per round, and average percent of correct buzzes.
