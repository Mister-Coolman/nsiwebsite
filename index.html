About Us:

The Niskayuna Summer Invitational is organized and managed by the Niskayuna High School Science Bowl team by Mihir Kulkarni, President of Niskayuna Science Bowl. Niskayuna High School is located in a small, suburban town in upstate New York in the capital region of New York. The word “Niskayuna” is a rough translation of the Mohawk word meaning “extensive corn flats.” Before Dutch settlers arrived, Niskayuna was a part of the Mohawk tribe of the Iroquois Confederacy which was a confederacy of 5 Native American tribes in New England which at one point extended through Michigan. Niskayuna High School Science Bowl is hosting the Niskayuna Summer Invitational in hopes of providing new rosters with the opportunity to participate in a competitive tournament before the official start of the new school year, and to further their own club’s ability to attend competitions.
